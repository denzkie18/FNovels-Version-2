As I working alone up to this point and create this website only using my android phone because I cannot afford to buy a PC or Laptop, the uploading and updating is a bit slower. I thankful for your understanding and patience for waiting. As I my self wanted to this website do not have an annoying ads but I'm afraid I cannot, because with the help of this ads, I gain a little bit of income that I plan to keep for recharging to the domain but unfortunately, even a year cannot earned at least 5 dollars. So I was hoping if I could gain a little bit of your support by donating any amount through GCash or Paypal.

If you want to donate, please send a message or contact me on my official Facebook Page. Thank you for all of you and hopefully you enjoy using my little fun made website. Thank you...
